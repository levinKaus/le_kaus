About Me

Hi, my name is Levin and I am currently a third year computer science and business economics student at Technische Universität Darmstadt.

During high school, I discovered my passion for computer science after taking my first IT class, where I was captivated by the limitless potential of this field. This interest was further piqued during a six-month internship at a startup, where I was introduced to the world of business economics, ultimately inspiring me to pursue combined studies including both disciplines at university.

Aside from academics, I enjoy travelling and exploring new places with friends and family. I also love watching movies at cinema and riding my bike trough nature.

In the future, I hope to be an entrepreneur and make a positive impact in the tech industry. I am excited to continue my journey as a student and look forward to what the future holds.
